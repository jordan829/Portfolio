About me

Hi, I'm Jordan Miller. I'm a computer science major at the University of California, San Diego. I am interested in computer graphics programming as well as UI/UX design. I have developed games and applications in OpenGL and Unity3D and have experience with integrating 3D user interaction. I also have experience with UI/UX research, design, and development. I am very passionate about the more visual side of programming and love taking on projects that test my spatial reasoning abilities. Check out my portfolio to see some of my work.
